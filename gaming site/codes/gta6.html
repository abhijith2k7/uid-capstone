About GTA VI

A New Era of Open-World Gaming

Grand Theft Auto VI is expected to be Rockstar Games’ most ambitious title, bringing a larger world, stronger storytelling, and a more realistic city atmosphere than ever before.

Vice City

A vibrant modern setting filled with life, chaos, and neon energy.

Advanced AI

Smarter NPC behavior and more realistic world interaction.

Huge Map

A massive open world designed for freedom, missions, and exploration.

Next-Gen Feel

Better visuals, atmosphere, physics, and cinematic gameplay.

From glowing nightlife and crowded streets to fast chases and detailed environments, GTA VI aims to create a world that feels alive in every moment.

More than just graphics, the real excitement comes from how the city reacts to the player, making every action feel immersive and memorable.
