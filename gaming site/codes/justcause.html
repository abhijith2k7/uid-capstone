About the Game

Just Cause is an open-world action game focused on chaos, explosions, and total freedom. Play as Rico Rodriguez and take down enemy bases using weapons, vehicles, and insane stunts.

The game features grappling hook mechanics, parachutes, wingsuits, and massive destruction environments where you can create your own action scenes.
